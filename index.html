City Stars

These are the stars that are visible in the night sky if you live in the suburban-urban interface—a 6 on the ... scale. The stars marked with red circles are the these familiar constellations:

  • Big Dipper
  • Orion
  • Scorpio
  • Cynus (the Swan)
  • Southern Cross (?)
They won't be recognizable until you travel to the sun's position and look at them from there.
